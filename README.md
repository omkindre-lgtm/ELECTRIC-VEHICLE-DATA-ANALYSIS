# ELECTRIC-VEHICLE-DATA-ANALYSIS

# INTRODUCTION

Electric Vehicle (EV) data analysis focuses on understanding the growth, adoption trends, and performance of electric vehicles using real-world data.
This project analyzes key factors such as vehicle types, range, manufacturers, fuel efficiency, and regional distribution to identify patterns and insights in the EV market.
The analysis helps stakeholders understand market trends, support policy decisions, and promote sustainable transportation solutions.

# KEY INSIGHTS

1) Electric vehicle adoption has shown a significant upward trend, indicating growing acceptance of sustainable mobility.

2) Certain manufacturers dominate the EV market, reflecting strong brand trust and technology leadership.

3) Battery range varies widely across models, directly influencing consumer preference and vehicle usability.

4) Urban regions show higher EV adoption due to better charging infrastructure and policy support.

5) Improvements in efficiency and range highlight rapid technological advancements in EV development.

# CONCLUSION

The Electric Vehicle Data Analysis provides valuable insights into the evolving EV ecosystem.
Data visualization tools like Tableau make it easier to identify trends, compare performance, and understand market behavior.
These insights can help manufacturers, policymakers, and consumers make informed decisions, contributing to the growth of clean energy transportation and a more sustainable future.
